人生 - Night Nature

I like Night Nature, because

  • Calm Atmosphere
  • Cool Air
  • Silence of Darkness

When I look at Night Nature photos, I feel like the atmosphere is calm and I hoping "I want to go there". I love night, because it feel cold and there is no noisy. Maybe my statement is not valid if you are in big city like Surabaya or Tokyo, that's why I choose forest for the photo.

I also love the view of cities in the night. The glowing building shine in the middle of the night. I start to thinking why the city is not fell asleep? Are the people stay awake for 24 hours everyday?

When I write this article, I start to imagining myself live in Tokyo, one of the most populated city in Earth. I hope I can live there one day. Find my love, Japanese wife and start my live with my little family.
